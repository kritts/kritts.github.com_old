Google (Streams, Photos, and Sharing)

This summer, I worked at Google at their headquarters in Mountain View, California. The overarching team that I worked for was Streams, Photos, and Sharing. This team is responsible for creating numerous Google products, including Google Hangouts, Plus, and Photos. I worked on the Android Toolkit team. My team consisted of around a dozen people who worked to develop a reusable framework for numerous Google products. I worked on developing Android animations and creating a library that made it simple for developers to use these animations within any of their software applications.
